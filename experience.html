So here I share fews meaningful experiences of my life.

1) Experience in Kayaking



      

Kayaking activities is a program under co-curricular. It was held for two days from 21 and 22 September 2018 at Pulau Melaka. It was my memorable experience because it was my first time kayaking and we need to kayaking for 20 kilometers.

2) Experience of Being Adopted

This program also my memorable experience because it is also my first time have foster family. It was held for three days which is from 23 to 25 November 2017 at Pulai Chondong, Machang. During programme, we are doing fun activities together likes zumba, gardening and playing netball. In addition, by participating in this activity, I has gain a lot of knowledge and skills and it can help me to increase my self-confidence and interpersonal skills because it teach me to getting involved with the community.

3) Zoo Negara Keeperku Programme

      

      

This program is part of my memorable experience because it was organized by my class (BA118 5B) and it was held last year on 22 November 2019 for subject Organizing Event. This programme require students to help the zookeepers in any approprite tasks such as feeding animal and clean the animal place. We were separated by the zoo officer into several group with different tasks. Luckily, my group was choosen to handle the Orang Utan areas.
